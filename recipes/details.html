Taco Recipe

Prep Time: 15 mins Cook Time: 15 mins Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • Protein
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Salsa
  • Cheese

Instructions

  1. Cool the protein.
  2. Dice the lettuce and tomatoes.
  3. Shred the cheese.
  4. Layer each ingredient in a taco shell.
